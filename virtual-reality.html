virtual reality

In technological understanding, virtual reality is a 3D computer-generated environment that is created to replicate the visuals similar to that of the real world. One can integrate a variety of images, videos and all sorts of media promoting or elaborating the products or services offered through their website in a single VR video. This will allow the users to seamlessly receive all the required information through a single experience. This will create stronger touchpoints with the least amount of time consumed. Although VR experience can eliminate the desire for taking a trip, in contrast it appears that most people after having the VR experience have a tendency to want to experience the destination in reality. Expertise in Different VR Hardware / Technology

Over the years our team has been successfully working in various VR hardware’s like Google Cardboard, Google Daydream, Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, Oculus: GO, Oculus Quest etc. This has allowed our team to gather a rich experience working with a diverse set of hardware’s and has refined their skills and proficiency level. With that they can now provide VR solutions in any existing VR devices & popular engines like Unity3D & Unreal Engine as per client’s requirements.

Ready Products in virual Reality

Our team has developed numerous VR applications over the years. These applications span across in house solutions as well as custom solutions tailored to fit client’s requirements. Currently we have a readymade solution for the following industries; AEC (Architectural, Engineering Construction) / Real-Estate industry, ART or Painting Gallery, Automobile industry, Color or Coating or Ceramics industry, Furniture Industry.
